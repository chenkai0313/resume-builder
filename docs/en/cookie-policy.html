Cookie Policy

Last updated: May 12, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how we use them on resbu.top, and how you can control them.

What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by a website. They help websites remember your preferences, understand how you use the site, and serve relevant content and ads.

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for three purposes: website functionality, advertising, and analytics.

Third-Party Cookies

Google AdSense and Baidu Analytics set their own cookies on our site. We do not control these cookies. For details, see Google's Cookie Policy at policies.google.com/technologies/cookies and Baidu's Privacy Policy.

Managing Cookies

You can disable cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers let you block all cookies, delete existing cookies, or block third-party cookies specifically. You can also opt out of personalized Google ads at adssettings.google.com. Note: disabling cookies will affect how Google AdSense ads are displayed, but will not affect the core resume building functionality.
