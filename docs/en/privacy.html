Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 12, 2026

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or process any personal information. All resume data you enter is processed entirely in your browser and is never sent to our servers. We use Google AdSense to display advertisements, which may use cookies to serve relevant ads. We also use Baidu Analytics to understand website traffic.

Cookies

We use cookies for three purposes: (1) Language preference — to remember whether you prefer English or Chinese. (2) Google AdSense — to serve personalized or non-personalized advertisements. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your previous visits to this website or other websites. (3) Baidu Analytics — to collect anonymous traffic statistics including page views, visitor counts, and geographic distribution. For detailed information about all cookies used, please see our Cookie Policy.

Third-Party Services

We use Google AdSense for advertising and Baidu Analytics for traffic analysis. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your browsing history. Baidu Analytics collects anonymous usage data. You can learn more at policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites.

Data Security

Since all resume data is processed locally in your browser and not transmitted to our servers, your data remains secure on your device. No registration or account creation is required. Your privacy is a core design principle of this tool.

Your Rights

You can disable cookies through your browser settings at any time. You can also delete cookies that have already been set. You can opt out of personalized ads through Google Ads Settings. For EU/EEA users: you have the right to access, rectify, and delete personal data under GDPR.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at privacy@resbu.top.
