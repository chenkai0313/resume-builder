Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 8, 2026

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or process any personal information. All resume data you enter is processed entirely in your browser and is never sent to our servers. We use Google AdSense to display advertisements, which may use cookies to serve relevant ads.

Cookies

We use minimal cookies for website functionality (language preference) and Google AdSense for ad personalization. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

Third-Party Services

We use Google AdSense for advertising. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your previous visits to this website or other websites. You can learn more about how Google uses data at policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites.

Data Security

Since all resume data is processed locally in your browser and not transmitted to our servers, your data remains secure on your device. No registration or account creation is required.
